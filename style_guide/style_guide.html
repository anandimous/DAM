DAM Asset Manager

Style Guide


Our Purpose

DAM is a recursive acronym for DAM Asset Manager. The project's idea was inspired by an idea from the Invenst idea bank. It will have a list of items available for students and professors[Clients].
They will be able to see item specifications and usage instructions. Clients will also have the option to reserve items for specified times.
