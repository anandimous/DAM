Dope Asset Management Library


Style Guide



Our Purpose

The Dope Asset Management Library was inspired by the idea from the Invenst idea bank. It will have a list of hardware available for students and professors[Clients].
They will be able to see their specifications and usage instructions. Clients will also have the option to rent them for specified times.
